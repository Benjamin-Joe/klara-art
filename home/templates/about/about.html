About Us

Question Answer
Who Are We? We are a small, family run business that believe in perfect, personal presents at a great price. We are a brand new business. But that's a good thing. We are here for one reason and that is to please our customers. We don't like juggernaut businesses because they are all about money :( We are about happiness, honesty and happiness. Notice how happiness is said twice. That's how focused on happiness we are :)
What Do We Do? Well that's simple. We sell art. I am a software developer in training and my wife is an artist. That's a pretty good combo if you ask me. She creates the art, I create the website. Our dream is to make as many people as possible happy with our creations. We sell everything possible (to go on a wall that is!) We hope that you will find something you like. If you don't find anything you like. Contact us, we love a challenge, send us an idea and we will make it for you :)
Where Are We? We are based in Sweden, But we are able to ship almost anywhere in the world. We cover the cost of shipping so you can pay less and save more :) We are looking to grow our business and expand to have a few small showrooms over europe. Or atleast that is the dream.
Where Can You Find Us? We have several online platforms on which you can contact us. You can contact us via the contact section at the sop of the screen. We aim to reply within 24 hours. You can also checkout the bottom of the page for our social media links. You can contact us directly on facebook and instagram. You can also see what is going on and keep more up to date with new projects and release dates.
Why We Don't Charge Shipping We want customers to enjoy our pieces, and something that customers don't like are extra hidden fees. So we removed that, What you see is what you pay. That way it is a little more simple and straight forward, and you are able to focus on what you want instead of rising costs of delivary.
What Happens If You Are Not Happy With Us? If you arent happy with us, then we aim to fix it. Please don't hesitate to get in touch as we are more than happy to solve any and all issues you may have. Up to including refunds and possibly even a free item or two for any troubles. We hope that everyone is happy with us all the time. But we also like to have a plan for incase things go wrong. So, if something doesn't arrive or arrives broken or you just arent happy with the item you have purchased. GET IN TOUCH !!!! :) We will fix it
